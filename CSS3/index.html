Community Bulletin

The city council is excited to announce a series of community events planned throughout June 2026. Residents are encouraged to participate actively in all activities to foster a stronger sense of belonging and togetherness in our neighbourhood.

The Music Festival kicks off the month on June 10th at Central Park. Local artists and bands will perform across three stages. Entry is available at ₹100 per person and includes food vouchers. Families with children under 12 get free entry.

The Food Fair on June 15th brings together over 40 local vendors showcasing cuisines from across the region. The Town Square will be transformed into an open-air dining experience with live cooking demonstrations throughout the day.

Sports Meet on June 20th welcomes athletes of all ages. Events include athletics, badminton, chess, and swimming. Register early as seats are limited to 100 participants. Winners receive trophies and gift vouchers sponsored by local businesses.

The Cultural Night on June 30th at the Auditorium is the grand finale event of the month. Featuring dance performances, cultural exhibitions, and a community awards ceremony. Tickets at ₹200 are selling fast — register on the portal today.
